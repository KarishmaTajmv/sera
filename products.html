We share pattern options (herringbone, stacked, brick, etc.) and grout color suggestions.

Lead time varies by series; we coordinate production and shipping to match site schedules.

Do you supply technical sheets?

Yes—spec sheets and test reports are available for each collection on request.

Need a tailored selection for your project?

Tell us your space, finishes you like, and timeline—we’ll shortlist suitable series, share samples, and assist with logistics.
